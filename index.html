Maricopa Polling

The reduced number and questionable placement of polling locations during the latest Presedential Preference election in Maricopa County elicited accusations of election fraud and voter disenfranchisement, especially in poorer and minority-majority communites. This map shows polling locations for the past three Presedential Preference elections as well as block level race statistics.
