Maricopa Polling

The reduced number and questionable placement of polling locations during the latest Presidential Preference election in Maricopa County elicited accusations of election fraud and voter disenfranchisement, especially in poorer and minority-majority communities. This map shows polling locations for the past three Presidential Preference elections as well as block level race statistics.

Predictive Visual Analytics Browser

As part of a survey paper on the state of predictive visual analytics out of the VADER Lab, this browser was created to showcase the papers we will discuss. As opposed to the more conventional approach of an extensive, manual review of the literature, we analyzed the entire body of work out of several relevant journals and used topic modeling to initially select the relevant papers.
